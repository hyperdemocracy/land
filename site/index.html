Hyperdemocracy is a series of lessons, speeches, programs, and productions - made by coders challenged by the many problems of public policy.

Our opening session on July 24, 2023 had around a dozen audience members. Some came to learn new ML skills. Some came for more exposure to processes of legislation and group decision-making. All of us learned a bunch, and are eager to keep pushing the dialogue ahead.
